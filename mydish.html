Recipe of Dhokla

Ingredients to make Dhokla

  • 1 cup gram flour(besan)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon refined oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 11/2 cup water
  • 13/4 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 15 curry leaves

steps to make the dish

  1. Prepare the Dhokla batter and let it ferment for 1-2 hrs
  2. Cook tha batter for 15-20 minutes in the idli/Dhokla cooker
  3. Prepare the tempering
  4. Serve the moist Khaman Dhokla with Faafda and Jalebi
